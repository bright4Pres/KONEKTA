KONEKTA STUDENT QUICK GUIDE

Welcome to KONEKTA.
This game helps you practice reading and understanding words and stories.

HOW TO START
1) Open KONEKTA.
2) Choose your student profile.
3) Walk on the map using Arrow Keys or WASD.
4) Press Space near a game area to enter.
5) Pick your language.
6) Answer questions by pressing 1, 2, 3, or 4.
7) At the end, press Enter to save your score.

IMPORTANT TIPS
- If you forget to press Enter at the end screen, your score may not save.
- If you are done playing, press Esc to return to the map.
- Ask your teacher for help if you see NO QUESTIONS FOUND.

GAME CONTROLS (SIMPLE)
- Move: Arrow Keys or WASD
- Run: Shift
- Enter game area: Space or E
- Return to map: Esc

DO YOUR BEST
- Read each question carefully.
- Take your time.
- Learning is more important than speed.
